So you've found my website. Congrats.

My name is Paul. I do research on AI & Security at Fraunhofer AISEC and I'm just starting my PhD at TUM under Prof. Dr. Claudia Eckert. This is supposed to be a place where you can get free access to my publications (no paywalls), as well as to my new blog, where I plan to post at least one blog each month. Not necessarily about tech, might be coffee or films or whatever I find interesting at the moment.

Research Journey

I started my research journey working with adversarial machine learning, focusing on physical adversarial examples. I've also worked in the area of Privacy of GenAI with a focus on tabular data, and my PhD focuses on dealing with uncertainty in large language models.

My academic background includes both Bachelor's and Master's in Informatics at TUM, but I also have experience with electrical engineering from my high school days.

Current Work

At Fraunhofer AISEC and TUM, I work on:

  • → Uncertainty quantification in large language models
  • → Privacy-preserving generative AI
  • → Adversarial machine learning

Outside Work

I really enjoy making coffee and pretending to be a home barista, although my latte art always sucks. When I'm not researching or brewing coffee, you might find me watching obscure films or working on random side projects.
